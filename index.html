How to Travel Between Cities
With Eurail vs Point-to-Point

For this exact route, point-to-point tickets booked 4–6 weeks ahead beat the Eurail pass by roughly €200 for 2 people. Here's the honest comparison.

🎫 Eurail Global Pass

Flexibility · No planning stress
  • 5-day Flex pass covers all major legs — use days only when needed
  • Germany & Austria: hop on any train, zero reservation fee
  • Switzerland: covered, no reservation fee on most trains
  • Paris TGV: pass valid, compulsory reservation ~€12 pp extra
  • Jungfraujoch: 25% discount — saves CHF 55 pp
  • Use Bayern Ticket & Einfach-Raus for short hops, save pass days
€636 for 2 people · 2nd class 5-day flex
Worth it if you don't want to plan. The Jungfraujoch discount alone saves CHF 110 for 2 people — partially offsets pass cost.

Cheapest · Book 4–6 weeks ahead

~€430 for 2 people · all legs booked early

The Details That Matter
Tips That Actually Save Money & Stress
✈️

Fly Condor from Toronto

Condor (German charter airline) consistently has the cheapest YYZ → MUC one-way fares. Check momondo.ca on Sunday mornings — Sunday bookings are 6–13% cheaper. Book 2–3 months ahead. Icelandair is the backup option.

🍺

Oktoberfest Survival Guide

Weekday before 2pm = find a table. Weekend or after 4pm = near impossible without reservations made months ahead. Each Mass (1 litre) is €14–16. Budget €50–60 pp for the full Oktoberfest experience. Wear comfortable shoes — the tents get packed.

🏨

Book Hallstatt Early

Hallstatt has fewer than 800 permanent residents and extremely limited accommodation. If you ever consider staying overnight instead of day-tripping, book 6+ months ahead. For the day trip, just book the first morning train to arrive before 9:30am.

🎟

The Bayern Ticket Secret

€44 covers TWO people for a FULL DAY on every regional train and bus in Bavaria — including the cross-border train to Salzburg, Austria. Buy it at a Munich station machine or online on weekdays before travel. Valid from 9am on weekdays, midnight on weekends.

🥗

Supermarket Picnic Strategy

Germany: Rewe · Austria: Spar or Hofer · Switzerland: Migros or Coop · France: Monoprix or Carrefour. A romantic couple's picnic — baguette, local cheese, charcuterie, a bottle of regional wine — costs €12–18 total and beats any restaurant view.

🌅

The Golden Hours Rule

Every major sight on this trip is best at dawn or dusk: Königssee before 9am, Hallstatt after 6pm, Salzburg fortress at sunset, Lauterbrunnen valley in morning mist, Paris from Trocadéro at 10pm. Build your days backwards from these moments.
