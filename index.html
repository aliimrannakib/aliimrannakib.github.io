💶 Currency & payment tips – to keep your budget on track
• Cash is king for small purchases – gondola (€80‑100), market stalls, bakeries, small cafés.
• Cards are widely accepted in hotels, large restaurants, supermarkets (Lidl, Aldi, Coop, Monoprix), and for train tickets.
• Best way to get cash – withdraw locally from a bank ATM (avoid Euronet / independent ATMs with high fees).
• Typical cash needed per day – €20‑30 per person for small expenses (coffee, gelato, tips, bus tickets where card isn't accepted).
• Always have some coins for public toilets (€0.50‑1) and small tips.
• Notify your bank before travel to avoid card being blocked.
