🧖 Mid-Trip Spa Reset · 2 Relaxing Sessions

🛁 Spa 1: Watzmann Therme, Berchtesgaden

📍 Address: Bergwerkstraße 54, 83471 Berchtesgaden, Germany
🕐 Opening Hours: Daily 9am – 10pm (Sauna area: 1pm – 10pm)
💰 Price: €12-15 per person (€24-30 for 2 people)
🧴 What to bring: Swimwear, towel (rent €2-3), flip-flops, water bottle
📅 Booking: No reservation needed — just walk in
🚆 How to get there: Bus 839 or 841 from Königssee to Berchtesgaden (10 min), then 5 min walk

🌐 Official Website → 🗺️ Google Maps →

🏔️ Spa 2: Public Thermal Baths, Zermatt

📍 Address: Staldenstrasse 45, 3920 Zermatt, Switzerland
🕐 Opening Hours: Daily 10am – 9pm
💰 Price: CHF 30-40 per person (CHF 60-80 for 2 people)
🧴 What to bring: Swimwear, towel (rent CHF 3-5), flip-flops
📅 Booking: Walk-in accepted; book online 1 day ahead for peak times (weekends 2-5pm)
🚆 How to get there: 5 min walk from Zermatt train station

🌐 Official Website → 🗺️ Google Maps →

💡 Spa Timing Tip: Go on weekday mornings (before 11am) for quietest experience. Day 5 after Jennerbahn = perfect for Watzmann. Day 8 (do nothing day) = perfect for Zermatt.
